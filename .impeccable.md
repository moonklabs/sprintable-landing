## Design Context

### Users
AI-native teams: founders shipping with AI agents, product teams running multi-agent workflows, studios/agencies proving process over prompts. Used during focused work sessions at a desk — evaluation mode, often late in the day. Context is professional, deliberate, technical.

### Brand Personality
Precision-obsessed, systems-oriented, quietly audacious. Not loud startup hype — more like a well-written spec. Sprintable is the ops surface serious teams use when AI work needs to be legible and accountable.

3-word voice: **machine-precise / human-legible / unapologetically opinionated**

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark theme — users are technical, sessions are focused, the product is about operations not lifestyle. But NOT the typical AI dark: no cyan-on-dark glow, no purple-to-blue gradients. Instead: rich near-black with warm undertones (towards indigo, not cool grey), large editorial typography, structured layouts with deliberate asymmetry.

Font pairing: **Bricolage Grotesque** (display — experimental grotesque with personality) + **Barlow** (body — technical, readable, compact). Both available on Google Fonts, neither appears in the reflex reject list.

Accent palette (OKLCH-based):
- Brand indigo: oklch(72% 0.14 265) — perceptually vivid without garish
- Teal highlight: oklch(78% 0.14 200) — secondary, used sparingly
- Surface: oklch(14% 0.015 265) — warm-tinted near-black, not pure black
- Border: oklch(25% 0.02 265) — subtle, keeps depth

### Design Principles
1. **Structure over decoration** — layouts should feel like a well-organized ops dashboard, not a marketing poster
2. **Legibility first** — type must be readable at every size; hierarchy should be unmistakable
3. **One thing per screen** — each section makes exactly one argument, nothing decorative added
4. **No AI slop** — ban: border-left stripes, gradient text, cyan glow on dark, identical card grids
5. **Earned asymmetry** — break the grid in one place per section for emphasis, not randomly

### Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing with glassmorphism cards and purple gradients
- "AI-powered" dark pages with neon cyan text on charcoal
- Card-heavy layouts where every section is just 3 icon+title+text boxes
